About Happy Street

Happy Street is a vibrant, interactive space where people come together to celebrate art, fitness, and connection. Organized to uplift communities, it offers a range of free activities that inspire creativity, health, and social engagement.

Join us for a variety of activities, including dance, yoga, games, and more. Happy Street is an initiative by The Times of India, encouraging a healthier and more connected lifestyle.

Happy Street transforms an ordinary area into an extraordinary venue, welcoming people of all ages. From yoga sessions and high-energy Zumba to exhilarating cycling races, there's something for everyone.

Live music fills the streets, with talented local artists performing to create a lively atmosphere. Sports enthusiasts can enjoy cricket matches, skateboarding, and traditional games that bring nostalgia and fun.

It’s not just an event—it’s a movement by The Times of India to encourage sustainable, non-motorized transport. People walk, skate, or cycle to the event, minimizing environmental impact and maximizing fun.
